### 2. Core Intake Questions

*   **Q1: The Intended Outcome (The 'Why')**
    *   What is the ultimate purpose of this process, product, or change? 
    *   Who is the primary beneficiary (e.g., customer, developer, HR candidate, underwriter, agent)?
    *   What value is unlocked or problem solved?

*   **Q2: Resources & Inputs (The 'What goes in')**
    *   What information, raw data, guidelines, inputs, policy rules, or starting materials are required to initiate this process?

*   **Q3: Operational Transformation (The 'What happens')**
    *   What is the conceptual step-by-step journey, transformation, or workflow that maps this input to the output (independent of any specific software, tools, or applications)?

*   **Q4: Boundaries & Invariant Constraints (The 'Limits')**
    *   What are the strict rules, regulatory policies, compliance guardrails, or boundaries that this process must NOT violate?

*   **Q5: Manifestation & Proof (The 'Success')**
    *   What constitutes a successful execution of this action?
    *   How will a human or system verify that the intent has been correctly manifested?
